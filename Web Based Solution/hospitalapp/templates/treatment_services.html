    Soft Tissue Surgery

    Soft tissue is the tissue that connects, supports or surrounds other body parts and organs. In addition, soft tissue includes tendon, ligament, membrane, fibrous tissue, fat, synovium, muscle, nerve and blood vessel. Therefore, the treatment of soft tissue diseases may require surgery, such as common soft tissue surgery, urethrostomy, perineal hernia repair.     Cardiology Department

    Cardiovascular system is an important organ to maintain life activities. It is mainly involved in the metabolism of blood circulation, so it is closely related to other systems. Although the primary disease of cardiovascular system is not many, it will inevitably cause dysfunction of other systems. Other system diseases often affect the function of cardiovascular system. Some pets of certain species, and those over 8 years old, are more likely to suffer from cardiovascular system. Healing Paws heart specialist is equipped with high-end equipment such as special stethoscope for heart, sphygmomanometer, digital X-ray diagnosis system, large-scale color ultrasound diagnosis system, as well as professional heart specialist medical team support to provide accurate diagnosis and treatment and intimate services for pets.
