    Sub-company in Chengdu

    Heating paws Chengdu pet diagnosis and treatment center is the first small animal general diagnosis and treatment center and referral center in Southwest China with international advanced medical equipment and professional service standards. The hospital process is scientific and rigorous, the diagnosis and treatment environment is comfortable and safe, and the dogs and cats are managed and treated in different areas, focusing on providing brand-new animal diagnosis and treatment experience and large-scale clinical application management. The diagnosis and treatment center gathers a number of senior small animal doctors for consultation, provides domestic professional clinical diagnosis and treatment services for small animals, and redefines the dignity and value of each small life.

    Sub-company in Shanghai

    Healing paws Shanghai Pet diagnosis and treatment center not only provides all-round pet health and diagnosis and treatment services, but also focuses on the development of animal bone surgery, neurosurgery and other characteristic departments. In order to better treat orthopedic diseases, we have introduced various kinds of diagnosis and treatment equipment, such as C-arm, ultrasonic bone knife, tplo bone plate system, PrCl advanced locking bone plate system, etc. At the same time, we also equipped with the rare MRI equipment in Shanghai pet hospital. With MRI, doctors can check the pathological organs in pets more accurately and improve the treatment accuracy.
